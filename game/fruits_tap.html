Fruits Tap

This is a simple tile tap like game
The fruits come down and just tap the circle to make them pop !
But be careful, don't tap the stones !
The fruits come faster and faster as the level goes up !
There are 46 kinds of fruits.
Can you see all the fruits ?

How to play

Tap the screen to start.
Tap the circle when fruits come near.
If you tap on the stone or you miss the fruit, you lose.
The game is over when you lose 3 times.
